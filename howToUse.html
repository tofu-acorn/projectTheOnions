Caramelized Onions: A Chef's Secret

Explain why caramelized onions are awesome before the list of uses

The extra oil

Explain that if the oil tastes weak (closer to unused oil out of a bottle) then use it with abandonment to cook with, but if it tastes strong then use it as a flavoring agent, like replacing the oil in homemade salad dressings, or hummus. Explain that no matter if the flavor is weak or strong, always adjust for the added salt.

Freezing

Once cooled, caramelized onions can be frozen. Freezing and thawing won't change the texture significantly, and the flavor will just as delicious. You can freeze them in an ice cube tray for individual servings.
